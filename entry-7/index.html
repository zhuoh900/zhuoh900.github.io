The Random Engine Generator lets you explore engines with different cylinder counts. Each engine type has its own characteristics:

  • 4-Cylinder: Compact and efficient, often found in daily drivers. Less power but great fuel economy.
  • 6-Cylinder: Balanced performance, smoother than 4-cylinder. Common in sports sedans and light SUVs.
  • 8-Cylinder (V8): High power and torque, classic in muscle cars and high-performance vehicles. Deep, aggressive exhaust note.
  • 10-Cylinder (V10): Rare, mostly in exotic supercars. High-revving, powerful, and thrilling to drive.
  • 12-Cylinder (V12): Ultimate refinement and smoothness, typically in luxury and hypercars. Produces immense power with silky operation.
