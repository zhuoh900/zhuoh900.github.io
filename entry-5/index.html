E92 M3

The E92 M3 is the V8-powered M3 produced from 2007–2013. It uses the S65 engine — a 4.0L naturally aspirated V8 that revs to 8,400 rpm. Rear-wheel drive, with either a dual-clutch transmission or a 6-speed manual. Known for sharp handling, big fenders, a power-dome hood, and iconic quad exhausts.

It is the only M3 ever powered by a V8, and the last naturally aspirated M engine of its kind. The end of an era before turbocharging. Clean design, high-rev sound, and still deeply loved by enthusiasts.
