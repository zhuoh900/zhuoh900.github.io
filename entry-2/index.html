Gear 2 — Supra & BMW B58

The Toyota Supra A90 and modern BMWs share the same 3.0L B58 turbo inline-six, but each brand tunes it differently. In the Supra, it’s sportier, punchy, and track-focused, while BMW emphasizes smooth, high-RPM refinement. Despite the shared engine, they deliver two distinct driving experiences, both legendary and highly tunable.
